Nov 13, 2025 Aula Theater UIN Ar-Raniry PRC

EDSA x EdUSA

Essay Workshop

Phillip Taw RELO Fellow at Universitas Negeri Surabaya

Education USA Event Partner

The session themed "Writing a Personal Statement that Gets Noticed" is designed to help students enhance their writing skills by focusing on clarity, organization, and effectiveness. This activity aims to guide participants in crafting well-structured and impactful personal statements for academic purposes, scholarship applications, or other relevant needs.

Led by Phillip Taw, a RELO Fellow at Universitas Negeri Surabaya, the session offers valuable insights and practical strategies, along with the opportunity for students to receive direct feedback to refine their work and make their statements stand out in competitive selection processes.

EDSA UIN Ar-Raniry dan EducationUSA Gelar Essay Workshop Hybrid Berskala Nasional

June 06, 2025 FTK B Hall, UIN Ar-Raniry Presidential

Lecturer Talk

Dr. Risdaneva, M.A Speaker

Fera Busfina Zalha, M.A Speaker

Akrim Mufaddhil Moderator

This event is designed to provide valuable insights for students, particularly those in the English Education (PBI) program, by highlighting the experiences of lecturers who have studied or taught in European countries. Through personal stories, the lecturers will discuss their educational journeys, the challenges of adapting to different academic systems, and the cultural experiences they encountered abroad. The event also serves as a platform to promote greater awareness of global educational trends and encourage participants to embrace the opportunities and diversity found in international education, especially in the context of increasing globalization.

EDSA Hadirkan Semangat Globalisasi Lewat Acara Lecturer Talk Bertema Eropa
