From Classroom to Global Stage

Public speaking is more than just talking; it is about connecting, influencing, and inspiring others. On November 13, 2025, EDSA UIN Ar-Raniry successfully held an International Seminar titled “From Classroom to Global Stage: Building Your Public Speaking with Confidence and Clarity”.

This seminar was designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Many students struggle with stage fright or anxiety when expressing their ideas in English. Through this event, participants learned practical techniques to overcome nervousness, structure impactful speeches, and deliver their messages with authority.
