About PRC

The Public Relations and Communication (PRC) Division develops relationships with students, teachers, and outside partners, manages EDSA'S communication plans, and enhances the organization's public profile. In order to expand EDSA's reach, it also works with other communities and organisations. On and off campus, PRC promotes EDSA's objectives and maintains transparency through partnerships, events, and the media.
