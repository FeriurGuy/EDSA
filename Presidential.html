About Presidential

Presidential is the officer in EDSA that being in charged of managing the administrations and associating with English Education Department. The President, Vice President, General Secretary, Vice of General Secretary, Cabinet Secretary and Treasurer work collaboratively with the ministries to empower English Education Department students.
